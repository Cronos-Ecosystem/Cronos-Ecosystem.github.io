ETH to lInea: What You Are Actually Doing

When you move ETH to lInea, you are bridging ETH from Ethereum Mainnet to Linea, a zkEVM Layer 2 network. This is not a swap between tokens; it is a cross-chain transfer secured by smart contracts.

Your ETH is locked on Ethereum and a corresponding balance is credited on Linea. The asset remains ETH, but it becomes usable within the Linea ecosystem.

You must start with native ETH on Ethereum Mainnet. Tokens on other networks cannot be sent directly to Linea using an Ethereum-to-Linea bridge.

  • Source chain: Ethereum Mainnet.
  • Destination chain: Linea (zkEVM).
  • Asset: Native ETH.

How ETH to lInea Bridging Works (Deposits, Finality, Proofs)

Bridging ETH to lInea involves locking ETH in a smart contract on Ethereum. After sufficient block confirmations, a corresponding amount is credited to your address on Linea.

The bridge interface may show pending while waiting for confirmations. The source of truth is the blockchain explorer, not only the UI status.

Deposits are generally faster than withdrawals, which require additional proof and finality steps on Ethereum.

  • Submit deposit on Ethereum.
  • Wait for confirmations.
  • Verify balance on Linea.

FAQ

What does ETH to lInea mean?+

It means bridging ETH from Ethereum Mainnet to Linea. Your ETH is locked on Ethereum and credited on Linea.

Is ETH to lInea a swap?+

No. It is a bridge between networks, not a token exchange.

How long does ETH to lInea take?+

It depends on Ethereum confirmations and congestion. Verify on Etherscan.

What fees are required?+

You pay Ethereum gas and possibly a bridge fee. Review before confirming.

Why is my transaction pending?+

Likely low gas or congestion. Check the transaction hash.

Can I cancel a pending transfer?+

If still pending, you may speed up or replace it. Confirmed transactions cannot be reversed.

Why is ETH not visible on Linea?+

Switch to Linea network and verify on explorer.

Is ETH to lInea reversible?+

No. You must perform a withdrawal back to Ethereum.

Should I use a hardware wallet?+

Recommended for larger amounts to reduce risk.

How do I verify the bridge contract?+

Cross-check the address on Etherscan with official docs.
