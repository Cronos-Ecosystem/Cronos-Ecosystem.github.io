Overview

Moving ETH to Linea means transferring Ether from the Ethereum mainnet to the Linea Layer 2 network. Linea is a zkEVM-based rollup designed to offer Ethereum-level security with improved scalability and lower transaction costs.

When you bridge ETH to Linea, your ETH is locked in a smart contract on Ethereum and a corresponding amount becomes available on Linea. You can then use that ETH for DeFi, swaps, NFT activity, or on-chain interactions within the Linea ecosystem.

The process is straightforward, but it involves two networks, two fee environments, and specific verification steps. Understanding how ETH to Linea transfers work helps you avoid mistakes and unnecessary delays.

Key Facts

  • ETH originates on Ethereum mainnet.
  • Transfer requires an official or trusted bridge interface.
  • You pay gas on Ethereum for the deposit.
  • ETH becomes usable on Linea after confirmation.
  • Linea uses a zkEVM architecture.
  • Funds are verifiable via blockchain explorers.
  • You must switch wallet network to Linea after bridging.
  • Withdrawals back to Ethereum follow a separate process.

How It Works

Linea is a zkEVM rollup. When you bridge ETH to Linea, your ETH is locked in a smart contract on Ethereum. The Linea system credits an equivalent balance on Layer 2. Security ultimately anchors to Ethereum settlement.

Process Stages

  1. Deposit transaction sent on Ethereum.
  2. Smart contract locks ETH.
  3. Rollup processes state update.
  4. ETH balance becomes available on Linea.

FAQ

What is ETH to Linea?
It is the process of bridging ETH from Ethereum mainnet to the Linea Layer 2 network.
How long does it take?
Timing depends on Ethereum gas conditions and confirmation speed.
Do I pay fees?
Yes, you pay Ethereum gas fees and later Linea gas for transactions.
Is ETH on Linea real ETH?
It represents ETH locked on Ethereum and is usable within Linea.
Can I send ETH directly to Linea?
No, you must use a compatible bridge process.
Can I use hardware wallets?
Yes, if integrated with your wallet software.
How do I verify the transaction?
Check Etherscan for deposit and Linea explorer for balance.
Can I move ETH back to Ethereum?
Yes, via the withdrawal process defined by Linea.
What if I use the wrong network?
Switch networks in your wallet and verify transaction details.
Where can I read more?
Consult Ethereum.org and official Linea documentation.
