What bridge ETH to Linea means in practice

Definition: To bridge ETH to Linea means locking ETH on Ethereum mainnet via a smart contract and minting or crediting corresponding ETH on Linea, a Layer 2 rollup network.

Mental model: Think of a bridge as a two-sided vault. Your ETH is secured on Ethereum (Layer 1), and a representation becomes usable on Linea (Layer 2). You are not “moving” coins physically; you are interacting with contracts that maintain accounting across chains.

Always confirm the bridge contract address in your wallet matches the official documentation before approving the transaction.
  • Transaction hash (tx hash): Unique identifier for your transaction.
  • Confirmations: Number of blocks validating your transaction.
  • Explorers: Etherscan for Ethereum, official Linea explorer for Linea.
  • Chain ID: Numeric identifier distinguishing Ethereum from Linea.

Step-by-step: transfer ETH to Linea safely

Step 1

Prepare your wallet

Ensure your wallet is updated and set to Ethereum mainnet. Confirm you control the correct address.

Check the public address in your wallet matches the one you expect before proceeding.
Step 2

Access the official Linea bridge

Navigate to the official bridge URL from documentation, not search ads or social media links.

Phishing sites often mimic bridge interfaces. Double-check spelling and SSL certificate.
Confirm the domain and certificate details in your browser.
Step 3

Connect wallet

Click connect and select your wallet provider. Approve connection request.

Verify the site requests only connection, not a signature unrelated to bridging.
Step 4

Select asset and amount

Choose ETH and input amount. Start with a small test transfer.

Ensure ETH (not another token) is selected and decimals are correct.
Step 5

Review fees

Check estimated gas fees on Ethereum and any displayed bridge fee.

Compare gas fee with current network conditions in your wallet.
Step 6

Approve transaction

Confirm the transaction in your wallet interface.

Never approve unlimited token allowances unless absolutely necessary.
Inspect contract address and value before clicking confirm.
Step 7

Wait for Ethereum confirmation

Monitor the pending transaction status in your wallet.

Open tx hash in Ethereum explorer and wait for sufficient confirmations.
Step 8

Switch network to Linea

After confirmation, change your wallet network to Linea.

Check chain ID matches official Linea documentation.
Step 9

Confirm ETH balance on Linea

Check wallet balance after network switch.

Validate receipt using the official Linea explorer and your address.
Step 10

Document transaction

Store tx hash and screenshots for your records.

Ensure both Ethereum and Linea explorers reflect completed status.
