What it means to move ETH onto Linea

Definition: Moving ETH to Linea means locking ETH in a bridge contract on Ethereum (Layer 1) and receiving a corresponding representation of ETH on Linea (Layer 2).

Mental model: lock on L1 → message validation → credit on L2. Your ETH is escrowed on Ethereum while Linea credits you on its rollup network.

How to verify: confirm the L1 transaction hash on the Ethereum explorer and check the corresponding credit transaction on the official Linea explorer.
